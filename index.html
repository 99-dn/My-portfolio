I'm Dan Nyarkoh Andoh — a Data & AI Automation Engineer based in Accra, Ghana, engineering the intersection of data pipelines and intelligent automation.

My work centers on architecting self-hosted AI automation systems with n8n, integrating LLMs like Google Gemini, Claude, and local Ollama models into production-grade workflows, and building the data infrastructure that makes it all reliable.

On the data side, I work with PostgreSQL, Python (pandas, sqlalchemy), and complex SQL to extract meaning from large datasets. I run my automation stack on Docker + Ubuntu 24.04, managing everything from Bash scripts to OAuth2 flows.

Currently expanding into Europe — learning Deutsch 🇩🇪 and Français 🇫🇷 with the goal of building my career in Germany or Switzerland.
