I'm Dan Nyarkoh Andoh — a Data Engineer based in Accra, Ghana, building data pipelines, engineering reliable infrastructure, and extracting insights from complex datasets.

My core stack centers on PostgreSQL for structured data storage and analysis, Python (pandas, sqlalchemy) for scripting and transformation, and Docker + Ubuntu 24.04 for clean, reproducible deployments.

I leverage n8n and AI models like Google Gemini, Claude, and Ollama to automate and enhance data workflows — turning raw pipelines into intelligent systems.

Currently expanding into Europe — learning Deutsch 🇩🇪 and Français 🇫🇷 with the goal of building my career in Germany or Switzerland.
